Platform policies
Effective June 1, 2026 · v4.2
1. User eligibility & registration
1.1
Minimum age
You must be 18 years or older to register. In jurisdictions where the legal age of majority is higher, that age applies. Accounts found to be operated by minors will be permanently suspended.
1.2
One account per person
Each individual may hold one personal account. Duplicate accounts are subject to permanent ban without appeal.
1.3
Real identity required
All users must provide their real legal name. Business names require a verified legal entity underneath.
1.4
Geo-restrictions
Vibel does not operate in OFAC-sanctioned countries. Users attempting to circumvent geo-restrictions via VPN will be permanently banned.
2. Listing rules
2.1
Accuracy required
Listings must accurately describe the item, service, or opportunity. Misrepresentation is grounds for permanent ban and potential legal referral.
2.2
Photos must be original
Listing photos must represent the actual item. AI-generated images are prohibited in listings. Stock images permitted for brand-new sealed goods only.
2.3
Pricing transparency
All prices must be stated clearly. Hidden fees, bait-and-switch pricing, and post-contact price changes are prohibited.
3. Enforcement consequences
3.1
Warning
Minor first violations receive a formal warning with a 48-hour cure period. Evidence retained permanently.
3.2
Temporary suspension
Moderate violations result in 7–30 day suspension. Active listings paused. Funds in Vibel Pay held pending review.
3.3
Permanent ban
Severe violations result in permanent termination. Device fingerprint, IP, and verified ID flagged to prevent re-registration.
3.4
Law enforcement cooperation
Vibel will comply with valid legal orders from any jurisdiction where we operate. Evidence packages delivered within 72 hours of a valid court order.
Prohibited items & content
Zero tolerance
Posting any prohibited item results in immediate removal, account suspension, and potential law enforcement referral. No warnings issued for items on this list.
